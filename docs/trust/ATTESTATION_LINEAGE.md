\# Attestation Lineage Semantics



\## Purpose



This document defines deterministic attestation lineage semantics within the pramanasystems governance ecosystem.



Attestations are treated as portable governance evidence objects.



Attestation lineage must preserve:



\- deterministic provenance

\- replay-safe execution continuity

\- immutable governance evidence

\- portable verification semantics

\- independently reconstructable lineage



Attestation lineage is treated as foundational governance infrastructure.



\---



\# Attestation Philosophy



\## Governance Evidence



Attestations are deterministic governance evidence generated during governed execution.



Attestations exist to provide:



\- execution authenticity

\- provenance continuity

\- deterministic verification

\- replay-safe execution evidence

\- portable governance trust



Attestations are not merely signatures.



They are deterministic governance evidence objects.



\---



\# Attestation Lineage Definition



Attestation lineage is the deterministic relationship graph between governance attestations.



Lineage relationships may represent:



\- execution dependencies

\- governance approvals

\- workflow transitions

\- trust transitions

\- release authorization

\- replay-safe execution continuity



Attestation lineage must remain independently reconstructable.

\# Core Governance Invariant



Attestation lineage must remain independently reconstructable from deterministic governance artifacts.



Lineage reconstruction must not require trust in:



\- infrastructure ownership

\- deployment environments

\- orchestration systems

\- centralized governance coordinators



Portable verification is mandatory.
